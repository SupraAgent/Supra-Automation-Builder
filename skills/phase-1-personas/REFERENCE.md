# Phase 1 Reference: Role Suggestions by Project Type

## Default Roles (every project)

- Product Lead
- Technical Architect
- UI/UX Lead

## Additional Roles by Project Type

| Project Type | Additional Roles |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Consumer app | Retention/Engagement Lead, Growth/Marketing Lead |
| SaaS product | Sales/Revenue Lead, Customer Success Lead |
| Marketplace | Growth Lead, Trust & Safety Lead |
| Content platform | Content Strategy Lead, Community Lead |
| E-commerce | Conversion Optimization Lead, Growth Lead |
| Developer tool | DevRel Lead, QA Lead |

## Persona Profile Sections

Every persona file must include:

1. **YAML Frontmatter** — name, role, company, title, triggers
2. **Core Identity** — name, title, company, years, background
3. **Expertise & Skills** — primary domain, secondary skills, methodology, tools
4. **Strategic Mindset** — core beliefs, optimization target, pushback items, decision style
5. **Perspective on This Project** — approach, key questions, red flags, success metrics
6. **Consultation Triggers** — 3-5 "consult when..." conditions
7. **Consultation Prompt** — ready-to-use prompt template

## Company Selection Guidelines

1. Look at comparable products from the project brief
2. Ask: "Who at [Company X] is responsible for the thing they do best?"
3. Research that person's background and approach
4. Prefer companies known for excellence in the specific domain
